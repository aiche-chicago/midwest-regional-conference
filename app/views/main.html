AIChE | Midwest Regional Conference
March 12 - 13, 2015


The AIChE Midwest Regional Conference (MRC) continues into its 7th year. Organized by the Chicago Local Section and Hosted by the Illinois Institute of Technology, the AIChE MRC provides an opportunity for engineers and scientists in the region to learn about new technologies and network with others in the region. A particular objective of the conference is to build technical relationships between industrial practitioners and governmental/academic researchers.
